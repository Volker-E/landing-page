News and outreach

As part of our commitment to open science and transparency, Wikimedia Research engages in a variety of outreach activities to share our work, project findings, and related research.

Follow Wikimedia Research

You can keep up with news and research activities of the Research team, as well as other research within the Wikimedia Foundation, through the venues below.

  • @WikiResearch: Wikimedia Research updates hot off the press.
  • Research Showcase: A monthly public showcase of recent research by Research team members, collaborators, and guest presenters from the academic community, hosted by Wikimedia Research.
  • The Wikimedia Blog: Announcements and reports about Wikimedia Research projects as well as research news from across the Wikimedia movement.
  • Research Newsletter: A monthly newsletter edited by Wikimedia volunteers and Research team members that features both internal research at the Wikimedia Foundation and other peer-reviewed research on Wikimedia projects.
  • Research mailing list: A public mailing list for discussions of research on Wikimedia projects.

For more information about news and outreach, including media inquiries, please email research-wmf [at] lists.wikimedia.org.
