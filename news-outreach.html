News and outreach

As part of our commitment to open science and transparency, Wikimedia Research engages in a variety of outreach activities to share our work, project findings, and related research. For more information about news and outreach, including media inquiries, please email research-wmf [at] lists.wikimedia.org.

Follow Wikimedia Research

You can keep up with news and research activities of the Research team, as well as other research within the Wikimedia Foundation, through the venues below.
